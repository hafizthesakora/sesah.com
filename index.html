Biography

Dr. Samuel Sesah is a Ghanaian optometrist who has made a significant impact in the field of eyecare in Ghana. He is the Chief Executive Officer of a nongovernmental organization called Sight at Home and Street Vision.

He has also gained recognition for his leadership skills and has held several leadership positions in various organizations. He his currently a candidate for the American Academy of Optometry Fellowship.
